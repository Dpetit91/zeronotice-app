Why Zero Notice Exists

Most people have a plan. A bag somewhere. A rough idea of what they'd do. What they don't have is practice executing that plan at 2am when adrenaline has replaced reason. Knowledge doesn't perform under pressure — execution does. And execution is only built one way: repetition without warning. Zero Notice was built on that premise. You define the protocol — your gear, your exits, your steps, your people. We fire the drill on zero notice, cut through Do Not Disturb, run the clock, and log your performance over time. Your response time drops. Your completion rate rises. When it counts for real, your hands already know what to do.
