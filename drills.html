How It Works

You define prepared.
We test if you are.

Most training apps tell you what to do. Zero Notice tests whether you can actually do it — on zero notice, under real pressure. The protocol is yours. You build it. You own it. The app just fires the clock.

Step 01

Build Your Prep

Open My Prep and define your emergency protocol — every item of gear, every action, every step. Add them in order of priority. This list becomes your drill checklist. It's entirely yours — no preset scenarios, no assumptions.

What Sets This Apart

⚡

No Preset Scenarios

Zero Notice doesn't assume your emergency looks like anyone else's. You define the protocol that fits your home, your gear, and your threat model. The app executes your plan — not a generic one.

🕐

Zero Warning

Drills fire at a randomized time within your scheduled window. No preview, no countdown. The whole point is that you can't prepare to prepare. You either know it or you don't.

📈

Performance Over Time

Every drill is logged — time, completion percentage, and delta vs. your personal baseline. The trend is what matters. Consistent improvement is the goal. The data shows you where you are.

🔕

Cuts Through DND

Real emergencies don't check your Do Not Disturb settings. Zero Notice uses iOS Time Sensitive notifications that bypass silent mode and Focus filters — so the drill fires the way a real alert would.
